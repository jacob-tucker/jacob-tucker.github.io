03. Experience

Decentology

Decentralized Applications Engineer & Smart Contract Developer

September 2020 - Present

I was lucky enough to begin my journey with Decentology in September of 2020, and I have been working here ever since. I am currently serving as the lead Cadence developer, which is the smart contract language for Flow. I am also very experienced in Solidity development, which is the smart contract language for Ethereum.

During my beginning stages at Decentology, I designed, architected, and implemented the smart contracts, transactions, and scripts for MotoGP Ignition. Since then, those contracts have handled hundreds of thousands of minted packs & NFTs on the Flow blockchain.

Additionally, I have helped develop our product DappStarter in hopes of achieving our company goal to onboard the next 10 million Web3 developers. DappStarter allows developers to quickly kick off their DApp development by providing them with an out-of-the-box full-stack application. The developer can select any blockchain and pre-existing module and they will have a fully working application in minutes, no pre-installed dependencies required.

Lastly, and most importantly, I am helping to build the Hyperverse, which aims to be the largest network of composable smart contracts for every existing blockchain. Up to this point, I have created a spec for both the Ethereum & Flow blockchains and hope to push out our first "modules" soon.

I will also note that the fantastic thing about being a part of a wondeful, kind, and supportive team like Decentology is that I have had the ability to learn many different aspects of the startup environment. From my deep knowledge of Solidity & Cadence, to product development, software architecture, and more, I am forever grateful to be here.
