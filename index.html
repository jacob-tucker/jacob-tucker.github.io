Hey there, my name is

Jacob Tucker.

I'm a software engineer.

I am an undergraduate student at Northwestern University pursuing a major in Computer Science.

After participating in the Open World Builders Blockchain Bootcamp in which my team and I built a capstone project to leverage loyalty rewards on the Blockchain, I was lucky enough to begin my journey with Decentology in September of 2020. I am serving as the lead Cadence developer, which is the smart contract language on Flow.

So far, I have written the smart contracts, transactions, and scripts for MotoGP-Ignition (https://motogp-ignition.com/), and I have helped develop DappStarter (https://dappstarter.decentology.com/), a platform that kickstarts developers in creating Dapps by providing out-of-the-box full stack code.

On the side, I have also developed full stack applications using React.js and Node.js since January of 2020, and since that time have completed multiple projects including a SpotifyAid, a search algorithm tool, and more.

Below is a list of the main languages and tools I have been working with recently:

• Cadence

• Solidity

• Javascript (ES6+)

• HTML5/CSS3/Sass
